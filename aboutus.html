About the Creators

Savvy Gupta

Savvy is a young and frivolous spirit trying to find her way in the world. She has many passions, some of which include: programming, singing, and eating. She wants to spend the rest of her life helping people and changing the world for the better, but for right now you can always find her inside, sleep-deprived and far away from sunlight.

Kim enjoys dancing in her free time, and will often spend up to fifteen hours a week at her dance studio. She has a long list of books she would like to read as well, but is unable to find time to finish them. At school, she focuses on math and biology, as they are her favorite subjects, and would like to become an engineer or doctor one day.
